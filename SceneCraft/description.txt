SceneCraft Addon for Blender - Elevate Your Architectural Animations
⚡ Create Stunning Procedural Animations with Ease! ⚡
SceneCraft is a powerful Blender addon designed to bring dynamic architectural animations to life. Whether you’re working on procedural floor animations, interactive motion effects, or realistic physics-based behaviors, SceneCraft provides an intuitive workflow to animate architectural elements with ease. This addon integrates Geometry Nodes, procedural animations, and customizable presets to streamline your creative process.

With SceneCraft, you can create stunning visual effects such as wave propagation, dynamic floor tiles, randomized motion sequences, and even bounce physics—all without the need for complex manual keyframing. Designed for both beginners and advanced Blender users, SceneCraft is a must-have tool for animators, architects, and designers looking to add realism and motion to their projects.

How to Use SceneCraft? (Quick Start Guide)
1️⃣ Install the addon (Blender Preferences → Add-ons → Install)
2️⃣ Select a floor or object collection
3️⃣ Choose an animation preset
4️⃣ Adjust speed, patterns, and physics settings
5️⃣ Click Apply and watch your scene come to life!
